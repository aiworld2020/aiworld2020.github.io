Gotech Bytes is a student run non profit educational and community service organization. Its mission is to spark student interest in Computer Science and Coding from the earliest grades through high school.

  • Students sharing their knowledge with other students.
  • Fun and interactive projects.
  • Coach students to be independent learners
  • Provide students with tools and techniques to continue learning beyond the classroom.

Frequently Asked Questions

All our courses are free. This is possible through our enthusiastic student body who love all things computer science.

Our classes are held through online video conferences and are taught by experienced youth instructors using a combination of slides, coding and projects.

Please review the syllabus for the course and date and time its offered. Use the Registeration form to register for the class. Video link will be shared with you after registeration.

We record all the classes to ensure quality. Recorded classes are for iternal use only to provide teacher training and review.

At this time we do not offer any make-up classes. Please reach out to your teacher for the lecture notes.

Of course. You should be enrolled as a high school student to be an instructor. Please submit your interest through Volunteer form. We will reach out to you in 3 business days.
