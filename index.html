Learn to Code ■ Build a Better Future

Gotech Bytes is a student run 501(c)(3) non profit educational and community service organization. Its mission is to build Education Equity in STEM by providing free Computer Science classes.

  • Students teaching other students
  • Offer FREE courses and education material with fun and interactive projects.
  • Spark student interest in Computer Science from the earliest grades through high school.
  • Coach students to be independent learners.
  • Provide students with tools and techniques to continue learning beyond the classroom.
